Hello!

You are a wisp lost in a forest. Isolated from your community, you are weak and exposed to the elements. You managed to conjure some of your power and light a fire, but you will not be able to do it again. Your only hope is to...

Keep the fire alive!

If you venture too far from the fire for too long you'll freeze. You'll be unable to keep the fire alive when dead...

  • WASD or ← → ↑ ↓ (or HJKL) - move around
  • E - pick up logs / place them into fire
  • SPACE (repeatedly) - chop down trees
